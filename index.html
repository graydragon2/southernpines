Southern Pines Archery

Southern Pines Archery is a USA Archery and S3DA program to teach kids and adults the joys of archery. Whether you want to shoot for competition or fun, come see us.

The USA Archery Program

Our USA Archery Program is open to anyone from age 8 and beyond. We will teach basic archery and safety to everyone. For those who wish to be more competitive we will also work with you on becoming a competitive archer.

For more information on USA Archery, please click the link.

The S3DA Program

Our S3DA Program is open to students in grades 3-12. Archers will learn indoor,outdoor, and 3d archery and will have the opportunity to compete both at a State and National level.
